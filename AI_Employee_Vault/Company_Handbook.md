## ğŸ“§ Email Automation Rules

### Auto-Triage Rules
- **Urgent Keywords:** urgent, asap, critical, emergency, important
- **Sales Keywords:** pricing, quote, proposal, interested, demo, trial
- **Support Keywords:** help, issue, problem, bug, error, not working
- **Admin Keywords:** invoice, payment, receipt, subscription

### Response Guidelines
1. **Sales Inquiries:**
   - Draft response with pricing information
   - Include link to calendar for demo booking
   - Mention key product benefits
   - Always require approval before sending

2. **Support Requests:**
   - Acknowledge receipt within 2 hours
   - Categorize issue type
   - Draft initial response with relevant documentation
   - Escalate complex issues to human

3. **Meeting Requests:**
   - Check calendar availability
   - Suggest 3 time slots
   - Include video call link
   - Require approval for new contacts

4. **Invoice/Payment:**
   - Log to accounting system
   - Flag amounts over $100 for review
   - Auto-acknowledge receipt
   - Update Dashboard with payment status

### Email Tone & Style
- Professional but friendly
- Keep responses under 150 words
- Use bullet points for clarity
- Always include signature with contact info
- Add: "This email was drafted by AI and reviewed by [Your Name]"

## ğŸ’¼ LinkedIn Automation Rules

### Content Strategy
**Post Frequency:** 3-5 times per week (Mon, Wed, Fri preferred)
**Best Times:** 9:00 AM, 12:00 PM, 5:00 PM (local time)

### Content Types to Auto-Generate:
1. **Business Updates** (Weekly)
   - New client wins
   - Project completions
   - Milestone achievements
   - Team updates

2. **Industry Insights** (2x/week)
   - AI/automation trends
   - Business efficiency tips
   - Technology commentary
   - Thought leadership

3. **Engagement Posts** (1x/week)
   - Questions to audience
   - Polls about industry topics
   - "What's your experience with X?"

### Content Guidelines
- Length: 150-300 words (LinkedIn sweet spot)
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Tag relevant people/companies when appropriate
- Add call-to-action (comment, share, DM)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include emoji sparingly (1-2 per post)

### Content Sources
- Completed projects in /Done folder
- Weekly CEO Briefing insights
- Industry news (web search)
- Business metrics from Dashboard

### Approval Requirements
- **Always require approval** for all LinkedIn posts
- Draft saved to /Pending_Approval/LINKEDIN_POST_[date].md
- Human reviews, edits, and approves
- Only post after file moved to /Approved
